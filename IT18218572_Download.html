Upload your MIDI file here

We need you! Without the help of the hundreds of musicians who upload their MIDI files we wouldn't be here. If you would like to contribute and share your MIDI files with your fellow producers, fill in the form below and submit your MIDI files.If you want konw how to do MIDI upload. It is very much appreciated! And if you like making MIDI files we'd love you on the team!.

Follow Instructions

1. Do not submit MIDI sequences by others unless you provide a means for contacting the author in order to obtain the necessary permission.
2. Only the following file formats can be uploaded : MIDI files (.mid), Zip archives (.zip-rar).
3. Please use only descriptive file names if possible, with no spaces (use underscore instead of spaces).
Example: malata_bambarekuse.mid.
4. Maximum allowed file size for a upload is 500 KB (KiloBytes).
If you have more issue or request,please go to comment

When you're done, hit the "Upload MIDI" button and your MIDI will be uploaded! If you like making MIDI files and would like to earn money by making premium MIDI files for us, get in touch! By clicking the "Upload MIDI" button you agree to the terms of service(+) for uploading MIDI files in to our website.
